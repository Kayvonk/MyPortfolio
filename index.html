About Me

     Originally from North Carolina, my interests led me to studying music at the University of North Carolina at Asheville. After receiving a BFA in 2015, I chose to study the sciences with the intention of finding a profession that could build upon my career opportunities. Since then, I moved to Oregon where I studied at Portland Community College and Portland State University until 2019. Currently, I reside in Utah, where I study computer programming with the aims of using those skills to develop educational tools to help make learning fun and emmersive.
